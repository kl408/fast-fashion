Why shop second hand?

  • You might find something valuable
  • Thrift stores help the community
  • They keep more out of landfills
  • You can create your own style
  • Your clothes have a story
  • You never know what you'll find!


















Create a zero waste wardrobe

Places that readily take donations of clothing or purchase consignment from you have various policies to ensure that the second hand clothing is wearable. No rips, stains, holes, etc. So when you shop at these stores the items are made sure to be clean and in good condition. Although second hand clothing can be from a few seasons back, they are items that can be repaired and reworked with your current closet. After all, fashion is a cycle and always recurring so it will one day be back in style. If you love the item, don't let trends stop you!

Why donate your clothes?

  • Helps those who are less fortunate
  • Keeps your wardrobe organized
  • Helps disaster victims
  • Promotes a less wasteful life
  • You can change someones life!


















Add to someone else's wardrobe

You can also donate to several shelters located throughout New York City. From socks to shirts to pants, those who are less fortunate struggle to afford essential basics. Once freezing temperatures hit, the request for winter jackets begin to skyrocket. Not only can you avoid adding to landfills, you can make it easier for someone to get through the day.
